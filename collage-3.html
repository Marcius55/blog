Laurenz Coninx was born in 1986 in Wilrijk. He’s a Belgian painter based in Mechelen. Armed with a Kodak camera, he captures various moments of his daily life, whether it's a bouquet of flowers or friends enjoying a party outside. Laurenz uses photographs as a source of inspiration for his paintings, transferring them onto canvas. A colourful palette, filled with oil paints and pastel techniques, results in a unique personal style with a joyful and happy mood "I want to be more positive. And I think that positivity is the driving force". He converted one of the floors of his house into his own painting studio, where he transfers his photographs onto canvas. Laurenz’s colorful works are sought after by many buyers who want to decorate their restaurants, offices, or home living rooms with the painter's works. His works are also published in foreign galleries, the most famous exhibitions in Belgium, and appear in the pages of art magazines.
