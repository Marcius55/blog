Variety Of Human Stories by Aistis Pučinskas

About me

My name is Aistis. I am Lithuanian and currently studying international journalism in Mechelen, Belgium. I come from a family of teachers and artists in a small Lithuanian town and spent most of my life surrounded by forests. The street where my parents' house is located is called Strazdelio Street, which translates from Lithuanian as “blackbirds street.” There were also plenty of blackbirds in our yard, which is what inspired me to name this page “The Chirping Blackbird,” as they constantly brought news to our yard.

Long journeys and hesitations finally brought me here to Mechelen. All my experiences so far have made me realize that I am truly inspired by other people's stories, their unexpectedness, uniqueness, and sometimes even their simplicity. Since the beginning of our humanity, people have shared stories, created them, retold them, learned from them, and, inspired by them, created their own stories. My goal is to share these stories with you and leave a little message in each one. I do what makes my heart happy and invite everyone to join me on my journey.
